Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 cups glutinous rice flour
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ube halaya (or purple yam flavoring)
  • 1/2 cup langka (jackfruit), mashed or chopped
  • Food coloring (purple and yellow/orange), if needed
  • Latik or toasted coconut for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine glutinous rice flour, coconut milk, condensed milk, and sugar.
  2. Mix well until smooth.
  3. Divide the mixture into three equal parts:
    • Part 1 (purple): Mix with ube halaya or ube flavor + purple coloring.
    • Part 2 (yellow): Mix with langka and yellow/orange coloring.
    • Part 3 (white): Leave plain.
  4. Grease a round or rectangular pan.
  5. Pour the purple layer in first and steam for 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  6. Gently pour in the yellow layer and steam again for another 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  7. Finally, add the white layer on top and steam for 15‚Äì20 minutes until fully set.
  8. Let the sapin-sapin cool completely before slicing.
  9. Top with latik or toasted coconut.
  10. Slice into squares or wedges, and serve!
‚≠ê Pro Tip: For vibrant colors, use natural ube and langka, and always steam each layer until set before adding the next!
