Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 6‚Äì8 pieces of saba bananas (ripe but firm), sliced lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup ripe jackfruit (langka), cut into strips
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 8‚Äì10 pieces of lumpia wrappers
  • Oil for frying
  • Optional: extra brown sugar for caramel coating

Instructions

  1. Slice the bananas in half or lengthwise.
  2. Roll each slice in brown sugar until coated.
  3. Add strips of jackfruit on top of each banana slice.
  4. Place the banana-jackfruit filling on a lumpia wrapper.
  5. Fold and roll like a burrito, sealing the edge with a bit of water.
  6. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  7. Carefully place the turon in the oil, seam-side down.
  8. Fry until golden brown and crispy.
