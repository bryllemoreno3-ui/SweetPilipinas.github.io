What is the difference between Leche flan and creme caramel?

Leche flan, for one, does not use cream. It mainly relies on evaporated milk and condensed milk for the creamy flavor. Creme caramel tastes lighter, while Leche flan is richer and denser. Leche flan is also traditionally steamed, not baked.

Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 10 pieces eggs (yolks only)
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  • 1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Separate the yolks from the egg whites (use only yolks).
  2. Beat the egg yolks gently, then add condensed milk and mix well.
  3. Pour in fresh/evaporated milk and vanilla. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Caramelize sugar in a llanera (flan mold) over low heat until golden brown.
  5. Spread caramel evenly, let cool, then pour in the egg-milk mixture.
  6. Cover with foil and steam for 30‚Äì35 minutes.
  7. Cool, refrigerate, and serve chilled. Enjoy!
