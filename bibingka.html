Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 slices salted egg, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Grated cheese (cheddar or queso de bola)
  • Fresh banana leaves (or baking paper)
  • Extra butter or margarine for brushing
  • Grated fresh coconut (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Lightly heat the banana leaves to make them flexible.
  2. Line muffin tins or round cake pans with the banana leaves and brush with butter.
  3. In a large bowl, combine rice flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Add in the eggs, coconut milk, and melted butter. Whisk until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pans or molds.
  6. Top with slices of salted egg and a sprinkle of grated cheese.
  7. Bake at 375¬∞F (190¬∞C) for 20‚Äì25 minutes, or until golden and cooked through.
  8. For a toasted finish, broil for the last 2 minutes‚Äîbut watch closely!
  9. Brush with butter and sprinkle with grated coconut if desired.
‚≠ê Pro Tip: For extra aroma, use real banana leaves and toast them lightly before lining your pan!
