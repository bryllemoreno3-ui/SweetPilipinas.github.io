Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • ¬º tsp salt

For the filling:

  • ¬Ω cup muscovado sugar
  • 1 tbsp flour (to prevent sugar from melting too fast)
  • Optional: pinch of ground sesame seeds or cinnamon

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt.
  2. Add oil or melted butter, then slowly add water until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead until smooth. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  4. Mix muscovado sugar with a bit of flour and optional sesame seeds or spices.
  5. Divide dough into small balls (about golf-ball size).
  6. Flatten each ball into a small circle.
  7. Place a spoonful of sugar filling in the center, fold the edges over, and seal.
  8. Gently flatten again with a rolling pin until thin and round.
  9. Heat a pan (no oil needed).
  10. Toast each Piaya over medium heat for about 1‚Äì2 minutes per side, until brown spots appear and the sugar inside starts to melt.
