Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened garbanzos (chickpeas)
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • 1/4 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened saba bananas (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka)
  • 1/4 cup macapuno (coconut sport strings)
  • 1/2 cup ube halaya (purple yam jam)
  • 1/2 cup leche flan, sliced into cubes
  • 2 cups crushed or shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • Optional toppings: pinipig (pounded rice flakes), ice cream (ube or mango)

Instructions

  1. In a tall glass or bowl, layer the sweetened fruits and jellies at the bottom.
  2. Add a generous heap of crushed or shaved ice.
  3. Drizzle with evaporated milk until the ice starts to glisten.
  4. Top with a spoonful of ube halaya, a slice of leche flan, and a scoop of ice cream if desired.
  5. Sprinkle with pinipig for a crunchy finish.
  6. Mix before eating‚Äîas the name says, halo-halo!
‚≠ê Pro Tip: For extra cold halo-halo, chill your glass before assembling!
