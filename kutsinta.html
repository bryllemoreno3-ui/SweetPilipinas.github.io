Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons tapioca flour (or cornstarch)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon lye water (lihiya) ‚Äì gives kutsinta its chewy texture

Instructions

  1. Mix all-purpose flour, brown sugar, and tapioca flour in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually, stirring until smooth and lump-free.
  3. Mix in lye water and annatto powder (if using) for color.
  4. Pour mixture into small molds, filling each about 3/4 full.
