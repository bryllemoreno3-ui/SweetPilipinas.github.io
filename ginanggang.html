Ingredients

  • 6 ripe (but firm) saba bananas
  • 3 tbsp margarine or butter
  • 4 tbsp white sugar (or brown sugar for a deeper flavor)
  • Barbecue sticks or skewers
  • Charcoal grill (or stovetop grill pan if indoors)

Instructions

  1. Peel the saba bananas and skewer them lengthwise on barbecue sticks.
  2. Over medium charcoal heat (or grill pan), grill the bananas, turning occasionally until lightly charred and golden all over. This usually takes 5‚Äì7 minutes.
