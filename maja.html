Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 1 can (370 ml) evaporated milk
  • 1 can (300 ml) condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup whole kernel corn (drained)
  • Optional: latik or toasted coconut for topping

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine coconut milk, evaporated milk, condensed milk, sugar, and corn.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
  3. In a separate bowl, dissolve cornstarch in 1 cup of water until smooth.
  4. Slowly pour the cornstarch mixture into the coconut milk mixture, stirring constantly.
  5. Continue to stir over low heat until the mixture thickens to a pudding-like consistency.
  6. Pour into a greased or lightly oiled rectangular pan.
  7. Smooth the top and let it cool at room temperature.
  8. Chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
  9. Sprinkle with latik or toasted coconut before serving.
  10. Slice into squares and enjoy cold!
