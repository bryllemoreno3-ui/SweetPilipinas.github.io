Recipe & Ingredients

Ingredients

Fo the rice cake:

  • 2 cups glutinous rice (malagkit)
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 ¬Ω cups brown sugar
  • ¬Ω tsp salt

For the latik topping:

  • 1 cup coconut cream (or thick coconut milk)

Instructions

  1. Wash the glutinous rice thoroughly.
  2. In a pot, combine rice and water. Cook over medium heat until the rice is halfway done (still a bit firm but water mostly absorbed). Set aside.
  3. In a large pan or wok, combine coconut milk, brown sugar, and salt.
  4. Stir constantly over medium heat until the mixture thickens slightly and turns into a syrup.
  5. Add the half-cooked glutinous rice into the caramel mixture.
  6. Stir continuously (this is where the arm workout begins!) until the rice fully absorbs the syrup and becomes thick, sticky, and glossy‚Äîthis takes about 15‚Äì20 minutes..
  7. In a separate pan, simmer coconut cream until it curdles and oil separates.
  8. Continue stirring until golden brown curds form. These are your latik!
  9. Drain from oil and set aside.
  10. Transfer the cooked Biko into a greased dish or banana-leaf-lined tray.
  11. Flatten the top using a spatula and sprinkle generously with latik.
  12. Let it cool slightly before slicing into squares.
