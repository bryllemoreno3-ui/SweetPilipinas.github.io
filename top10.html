Top 10 Filipino Desserts

Savor the Sweetness of Filipino Culture!

Each dessert on this list is more than just a treat—it's a story, a tradition, and a celebration of Filipino heritage. Dive in and discover why these sweets have captured the hearts (and taste buds) of generations!

1
Leche Flan
A creamy caramel custard dessert that’s a Filipino favorite at every celebration.
Learn More
2
Halo-Halo
A refreshing mix of shaved ice, milk, and colorful sweet toppings—perfect for summer!
Learn More
3
Bibingka
A soft rice cake baked in banana leaves, often enjoyed after Simbang Gabi during Christmas.
Learn More
4
Puto Bumbong
Purple rice cake steamed in bamboo tubes, served with coconut and muscovado sugar.
Learn More
5
Turon
Sweet banana spring rolls coated in caramelized sugar—a crunchy, golden treat!
Learn More
6
Maja Blanca
A creamy coconut pudding topped with toasted coconut or corn—smooth and satisfying.
Learn More
7
Sapin-Sapin
A colorful layered rice cake made with coconut milk and sugar—each layer a different flavor!
Learn More
8
Buko Pandan
A refreshing dessert made with young coconut, pandan jelly, and sweet cream.
Learn More
9
Cassava Cake
A rich and chewy cake made from grated cassava and coconut milk.
Learn More
10
Kutsinta
A sticky rice cake with a chewy texture, served with grated coconut.
